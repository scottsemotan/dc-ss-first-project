Vegetable Stir Fry

Marinated tofu with stir fry vegetables in a soy, honey & orange juice sauce over a bed of white rice or noodles.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups Stir Fry Veggies
  • 1 package Firm Tofu, pressed and cubed
  • 1 teaspoon Organic Toasted Sesame Oil
  • 1/4 cup Orange Juice
  • 1/4 cup Soy Sauce
  • 2 teaspoons Clover Honey
  • 1 teaspoon Fresh Ginger, grated

Instructions

  1. Mix sesame oil, orange juice, soy sauce, honey and ginger.
  2. Add tofu and let marinate for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Cook stir fry veggies according to package directions.
  4. Add tofu to stir fry and cook for an additional 2 minutes.
  5. Add sauce to stir fry and heat through.
  6. Serve over rice noodles (or rice), if desired.
